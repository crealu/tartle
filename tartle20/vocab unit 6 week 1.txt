Mitochondria 
	- produces energy through chemical reactions,
		controls the level of water and other materials
		in a cell


Cell membrane
	- thin covering, gives teh cell its shape,
		protects the cell from the outside environment,
		controls movement in and out of the cell

Nucleus
	- the "brain" of the cell, in charge of making sure all
		other organelles do their jobs


Cytoplasm
	- jelly-like substance between the organelles inside the cell

Vacuole
	- stores food, water, and waste


Cell wall
	- thick, protective covering found only in a plant cell,
		supports and protects the cell


Chloroplast
	- "food factory", makes energy from the sun, then uses
		the energy to make food for the plant cell
